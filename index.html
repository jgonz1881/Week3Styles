Smite

Smite is my favorite video game because it combines mythology with competitive team-based gameplay.

About Smite

Smite is a third-person MOBA developed by Hi-Rez Studios. Players control gods from different mythologies and work together to defeat the enemy team.

Gameplay

Each player has a specific role that helps the team succeed.

  • Carry
  • Mid
  • Jungle
  • Support
  • Solo

Why I Like Smite

I enjoy Smite because every match feels different and learning new gods keeps the game exciting.
